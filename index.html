The Hunter's Cup — Official Rules
Koven Technology Q3 2026 · July 1 – September 30
Hunt new business, build your office, claim the Cup.
🎯
Contest Objective
1
The goal is to complete your personal office to 100%, obtain the Hunter's Cup trophy, and hold the Cup until the end of the competition on September 30, 2026.
2
The Hunter's Cup is an individual competition focused entirely on finding and developing new business. Only first-time customers qualify.
3
The rep with the most Hunter Points at the end of Q3 wins the Hunter's Cup and the grand prize. Monthly winners are recognized at the end of July and August with mini prizes.
4
Points accumulate across the entire quarter. Monthly standings are for recognition purposes only — they do not reset the overall leaderboard.
💡
The leaderboard updates the moment an item ships to the customer. Keep building — every new account upgrades your office and moves you closer to the Cup.

Prior monthly winners are not changed if probe sales are later corrected and reassigned after month-end. However, those corrected points will count toward the current and future months on the overall leaderboard.
🏥
What Counts as a New Account
1
A customer qualifies as new only if they have not purchased a Koven Doppler or probe within the previous 3 years.
2
If it is a new department within an existing hospital or health system, that sale may qualify — but it is subject to management approval on a case-by-case basis.
3
All qualifying new accounts must have clear, detailed notes entered in Goldmine.
4
The leaderboard updates once the item ships to the customer — not at the time of order entry or invoice.
⚠️
No Goldmine notes = no points. Accounts without notes documented in Goldmine will not be counted, regardless of qualifying status.
↩️
Returns & Adjustments
1
If a rep receives a return during the contest period, they will lose the revenue, points, and all associated leaderboard data tied to that sale — including account count, sale type bonus, and demo bonus.
2
Returns are processed immediately upon confirmation. The leaderboard will reflect the adjustment in real time.
3
A returned account that was previously credited toward a monthly win does not retroactively change that month's winner, but the points will be deducted from the rep's overall Q3 total going forward.
🚨
Returns cost you everything associated with that sale. Revenue, points, account count, bonuses — all removed.
⚖️
Tiebreaker Rules
1
First tiebreaker: Total revenue generated from new accounts to the current date. The rep with the higher cumulative revenue wins the tiebreak.
2
Second tiebreaker: Total number of new accounts to the current date. The rep with more qualifying new accounts wins.
📧
How to Submit a New Account
1
Email the new customer invoice to Carolyn and CC Stephen to officially track the new business. This is required for every qualifying account.
2
Ensure clear notes are entered in Goldmine.
3
Once the item ships and the submission is verified, the leaderboard will be updated and points will be awarded.

Office Progression & Hunter Levels
1
Every rep starts with an empty office with moving boxes. As you earn points, your office upgrades automatically — from a basic desk to an executive corner office with the Hunter's Cup trophy.
2
30 points = 100% office completion. Reaching 100% means you've built the ultimate office and earned the right to hold the Hunter's Cup. Keep earning points beyond 100% to show your dominance.
3
Office stages: 📦 Empty → 🪑 Desk & Chair → 💻 Computer & Phone → 🖼 Wall Décor → 🪴 Plants & Upgraded Furniture → 🛋 Executive Conference Area → 🏆 Corner Office + Trophy.
📋
General Contest Rules & Conduct
1
Points are awarded based on shipped orders only — not quotes, orders in progress, or unshipped inventory. The leaderboard updates upon confirmed shipment.
2
Contest runs July 1 through September 30, 2026. The overall Q3 champion is determined by total Hunter Points accumulated across all three months. Monthly champions are determined at the end of each respective month.
3
Have fun. The Hunter's Cup is designed to celebrate new business, recognize achievement, and build a culture of hunting. Help each other grow — the rising tide lifts all boats.
